Our Story

Born in Motion

Within the historic walls of the University of Ibadan, a different kind of legacy began to rise. De Ultimate Steppers was born from a shared belief among passionate dancers who understood that dance is more than rhythm and routine; it is identity in motion.

What started as simple training sessions on campus, driven by raw passion, late rehearsals, and unbreakable dedication, slowly evolved into a powerful collective known for energy, discipline, and unmistakable presence.

We do not chase trends; we refine craft. We do not imitate; we innovate.

Find Your Rhythm

Hip-Hop, Afro, Contemporary, and Breakdance shape the way we move, perform, and connect with every audience.

More than a dance crew, we are a family built on respect, consistency, and shared vision.

Our mission is clear: to inspire, to represent, and to elevate dance culture within and beyond the University of Ibadan.
